Operation: Ethereal Reconnaissance

Agent, your mission is to conduct a reconnaissance operation on Ethreal, a fictitious entity operating under unknown directives. Your objective is to gather intelligence using CeWL and analyze potential vulnerabilities.

Mission Objectives

1. Infiltrate the Target Network

  • Access the Ethreal website running on https://ethreal.com.
  • Confirm successful connectivity and reconnaissance parameters.

2. Deploy Intelligence-Gathering Tools

  • Execute a series of CeWL commands to extract key intelligence, including structured wordlists and email addresses.
  • Maintain operational security while conducting extractions.

3. Analyze Collected Data

  • Cross-reference wordlists for high-value keywords and domain-specific terms.
  • Evaluate extracted email addresses for potential security exposures.

4. File Your Intelligence Report

  • Submit all extracted wordlists and email data.
  • Provide a detailed analysis of your findings.
  • Recommend defensive countermeasures Ethereal could implement to mitigate intelligence leaks.

Mission Execution: CeWL Commands

Initalize the Lab

labtainer cewl

Visit the Target Site (Optional)

firefox ethreal.com

Review CeWL Options

cewl -h

Basic Wordlist Extraction

This command scrapes words from the homepage of the target website and prints them to the terminal.

cewl https://ethreal.com

Saving a Wordlist

The -w option saves the extracted words into a file for later use.

cewl -w wordlist.txt https://ethreal.com

Setting Crawl Depth

The -d option tells CeWL to follow links within the website. A depth of 2 means it will scrape the main page and follow internal links up to two levels deep. This is the default setting. Try scanning at a depth of 1.

cewl -d 1 -w depth1_wordlist.txt https://ethreal.com

Setting Minimum Word Length

The -m option filters out words shorter than a specified length. The default is 3 characters. Here, only words with at least 5 characters will be saved.

cewl -m 5 -w min5_wordlist.txt https://ethreal.com

Extracting Emails

The -e flag enables email extraction from the webpage, saving the results into a text file.

cewl -e --email_file emails.txt https://ethreal.com/contact

Including Numbers

The --with-numbers allows CeWL to include words that contain numbers.

cewl --with-numbers -w number_wordlist.txt https://ethreal.com

Intelligence Report: Analysis Questions

  • Compare the different wordlists you generated. Which provided the most useful data?
  • Were all emails successfully scraped? Why or why not?
  • How does setting different depth levels impact the extraction results?
  • How can CeWL be used in a real-world cybersecurity attack?
  • What defenses can websites implement to prevent scraping?

Final Submission

Deliverables

  • Extracted Wordlists (wordlist.txt, depth2_wordlist.txt, etc.).
  • Email List (emails.txt).
  • Mission Analysis Report answering the questions above.
